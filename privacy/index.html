Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 8, 2025

Privacy-First Design: Tamagrey is designed with privacy at its core. All Screen Time data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

Tamagrey collects and processes the following types of information:

  • Screen Time Data: Information about your app usage patterns, including which apps you choose to block and your focus session duration
  • Virtual Pet Data: Your pet's health status, focus achievements, and game progress
  • App Settings: Your preferences and configuration settings within the app
  • Usage Statistics: Daily focus time, session counts, and wellness metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively to:

  • Provide core app functionality including focus sessions and app blocking
  • Maintain your virtual pet's health and progress
  • Display your personal usage statistics and achievements
  • Preserve your settings and preferences

3. Data Storage and Processing

All data processing occurs locally on your device using:

  • Device Storage: Your data is stored locally in the app's secure container
  • App Group Sharing: Data is shared between the main app and extensions using Apple's secure App Group framework
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not store your data on external servers or in the cloud

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal data to third parties. Specifically:

  • Screen Time data never leaves your device
  • Virtual pet progress is stored locally only
  • Usage statistics are for your personal viewing only
  • No analytics or tracking services are used

5. Apple Screen Time Framework

Tamagrey uses Apple's Screen Time API and Family Controls framework to provide app blocking functionality. This integration:

  • Requires explicit user permission through Apple's authorization flow
  • Operates within Apple's privacy and security guidelines
  • Processes data locally using Apple's secure frameworks
  • Does not bypass Apple's built-in privacy protections

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device until:

  • You delete the app from your device
  • You reset your device
  • You manually clear the app's data through iOS settings

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app's interface
  • Deletion: Delete the app to remove all associated data
  • Control: Revoke Screen Time permissions at any time through iOS Settings
  • Portability: Export your statistics through the app's sharing features

8. Children's Privacy

Tamagrey is designed for users 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us immediately.

9. Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored in the app's secure sandbox environment
  • No network transmission of sensitive data
  • Compliance with Apple's App Store security requirements
  • Regular security updates through app updates

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • App Store update notes
  • Updated effective date on this policy

11. International Users

This app is designed primarily for users in regions where Apple's Screen Time API is available. All data processing occurs locally on your device regardless of your location.
